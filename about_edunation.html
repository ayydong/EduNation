About EduNation

Mission Statement

EduNation is a children’s education startup that designs, develops, and distributes multimedia options and supplements for the developmental enrichment of young children. Our company goal is to provide affordable, educational, and fun resources for children around the world, while supporting underprivileged education programs.

Partnerships

EduNation is a proud partner of Ontokids Technology – a multimedia children’s education platform based in Shenzhen, China – as well as NineFruits Academy – a bilingual tutoring firm in California – and the Palo Alto Unified School District; EduNation’s books are distributed at five public libraries in Palo Alto, and are packaged with Ontokids’s educational products for distribution in China.
